FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What types of wood are available and what is best for outdoor furniture?

A: Most woods can be used for outdoor furniture, however some varieties are naturally better suited for use outdoors. Cedar, Acacia, Douglas-Fir, Teak, and Redwood are a few common varieties used in American outdoor furniture.
